FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking with us.

How quickly can you come out?
For emergencies, we aim to arrive within 2 hours of your call, 7 days a week. For scheduled, non-urgent work, we typically have availability within 1–3 business days. You can also book online and choose from available time slots that work for your schedule.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes — all quotes are free and come with no obligation. For most jobs, we can give you a price estimate over the phone or via our contact form. For larger or more complex projects, we'll schedule a brief on-site visit to assess the work before providing a written quote.
Are your technicians licensed and insured?
Absolutely. Every member of our team is fully licensed in their trade, background-checked before hiring, and covered by our $2 million general liability insurance policy as well as workers' compensation. You can request proof of insurance any time — we're happy to provide it.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work?
Our 100% satisfaction guarantee means we'll come back and make it right at no additional charge. We take pride in our work, and we stand behind every job we complete. If something isn't right, call us within 30 days and we'll fix it — simple as that.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Metro City and the surrounding areas including Riverside, Oak Park, Lakeside, Northview, Elm Grove, and Cedar Falls. Not sure if we cover your area? Give us a call or check the map on our Contact page — we're happy to let you know and may be able to accommodate areas not listed.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), debit cards, checks, and cash. Payment is collected after the job is complete and you've approved the work. For larger projects, we offer flexible financing options — ask us for details when you request your quote.
